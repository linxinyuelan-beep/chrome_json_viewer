Privacy Policy for JSON Formatter & Viewer

Last updated: August 2, 2025

At JSON Formatter & Viewer, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use our Chrome extension.

Information Collection

JSON Formatter & Viewer is designed with privacy in mind. Here's what you should know about data collection:

  • We do not collect any personal information from you when you use our extension.
  • We do not track your browsing activity across websites.
  • We do not send any data to external servers or third parties.

Local Storage Usage

Our extension does use Chrome's storage API to store certain data locally on your device:

  • JSON History: The extension saves a history of recently viewed JSON data to enhance your experience by allowing you to quickly access previously viewed JSON content.
  • User Preferences: Settings such as your preferred view mode and feature toggles are saved locally.

All this data is stored exclusively on your local device using Chrome's built-in storage capabilities. It never leaves your browser and is not transmitted to us or any third parties.

Permissions

Our extension requires certain permissions to function correctly:

  • activeTab: To access and detect JSON in the current tab you're viewing
  • scripting: To format and enhance the display of detected JSON
  • contextMenus: To provide right-click menu options for JSON formatting
  • storage: To save your settings and JSON history locally
  • host permissions: To detect and format JSON across websites you visit

These permissions are used solely for the purpose of providing the extension's core functionality of detecting, formatting, and visualizing JSON data.

Data Removal

You can clear all locally stored data at any time by:

  1. Opening the extension popup
  2. Accessing the history panel
  3. Clicking the "Clear History" button

Alternatively, you can remove all data associated with the extension by uninstalling it from Chrome.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
